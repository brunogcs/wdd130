History

White Water Rafting (WWR) was founded with a passion for adventure and a deep respect for the rivers we call home. Inspired by the early pioneers of rafting — from the first rubber rafts of the 1840s to the guided expeditions that gained popularity in the mid‑20th century

What started as a small group of river enthusiasts has grown into a trusted outfitter, known for combining adrenaline‑filled rapids with breathtaking scenery and a commitment to preserving our waterways for future generations.
