White Water Rafting Co.

Propósito: Conectar pessoas à natureza por meio de aventuras seguras e emocionantes.

Missão:Oferecer experiências de rafting inesquecíveis com segurança, diversão e respeito ao meio ambiente.

Credo: Aventura,segurança, preservação e espírito de equipe. Lema: "Sinta a emoção, viva a aventura."

History

White Water Rafting (WWR) was founded with a passion for adventure and a deep respect for the rivers we call home. Inspired by the early pioneers of rafting — from the first rubber rafts of the 1840s to the guided expeditions that gained popularity in the mid‑20th century2 — WWR began offering thrilling, safe, and eco‑friendly rafting trips for all skill levels. What started as a small group of river enthusiasts has grown into a trusted outfitter, known for combining adrenaline‑filled rapids with breathtaking scenery and a commitment to preserving our waterways for future generations.
